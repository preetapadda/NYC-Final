New York Yankees


If you’ve been to New York before you know it gets super hot in the summer. And nothing says a New York summer like taking in a Yankee game with an ice cold beer in one hand and a hot dog in the other whilst chilling with some friends. So batter up and book those tickets!

Broadway


Big Lights. Big Stars. Big City. A visit to New York City isn’t complete without witnessing the spectacle that is Broadway. With over 40 shows, including musicals and plays, big name shows and small productions there is definitely something for everyone.
Get ready for the curtain call.

Top Tip: Queue up at the TKTS Booth in Times Square every day at 3pmto get half price tickets to all shows

Price: $-$$$

New York Rangers


Let’s go Rangers! Let’s go! First thing you need to know is ice hockey is mental. It is without a doubt the most exciting American sport. The atmosphere is comparable to that of a soccer (translation: football) game in Britain: intense and highly charged.

Price: $$-$$$
