Lets get wasted New York style...

Frying Pan


Ever wanted to have lobster and cocktails on a historic vessel permanently docked on the Hudson River? Look no further than the Frying Pan. This unusual bar setting is perfect for afternoon cocktails on a lazy summer weekend.

Price: $$$

230 Fifth


For a classy rooftop bar with an insane view of the New York skyline check out 230 Fifth bar. With an extensive cocktail list and many bar snacks to choose from this place is a favourite for after work drinks.

Price: $$

The Stumble Inn


For a classic dive bar atmosphere reminiscent of a college frat party this is a great bar for the 20 something tourist wanting to mingle with post-college Americans. Whether its beer pong or taking in one of the classic American sports games this place will surely make for a forgettable night :P

Price: $
